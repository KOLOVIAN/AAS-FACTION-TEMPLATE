// Ops briefing
  1. Set your Template name // at the top — this becomes _name in the SQF. Don't leave it as TEMPLATE_NAME.
  2. Expand any section to override its defaults. Anything you don't touch stays at vanilla NATO values.
  3. Need a custom loadout? Paste the full Eden array (with the standard "" escapes) into the classname field — the page detects it, marks it [ARRAY], and doubles the quotes for you.
  4. Hit COPY TEMPLATE at the bottom. Drop the result into the init field of an invisible helipad in your mission, then place that helipad somewhere accessible.
  5. Loading the mission as host fires the script once, installs the template into profileNamespace, and the helipad deletes itself. Open the AAS pager to verify the load notification.
